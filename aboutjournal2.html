Focus and Scope

The University of Manitoba Journal of Medicine (UMJM) is looking for pieces relating to medical science, clinical or surgical medicine, medical ethics, medical humanities, narrative medicine, hstory of medicine, epidemiology, healthcare, social determinants of health, and global health. Proposals on other topics are certainly welcomed as well.
